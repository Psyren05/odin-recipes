Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This chocolate chip cookie recipe makes delicious cookies with crisp edges and chewy middles.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • white sugar
  • brown sugar
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • all-purpose flour
  • chocolate chips

Steps

  1. 1. Gather ingredients
  2. 2. Preheat oven
  3. 3. Beat butter and both sugars
  4. 4. Add eggs and vanilla
  5. 5. Add baking soda and salt
  6. 6. Stir in flour and chocolate chips
  7. 7. Shape dough into balls and place on baking sheet
  8. 8. Bake
  9. 9. Eat!
